Hello! My name is Kelly Alvarez.

As for my involvement in extracurriculars, I am currently a part of four clubs at my school: student council, audio/video club, art club, and book club. Now, given the opportunity to learn coding, I aim to enrich my knowledge of such innovation in order to help improve the lives of others.
